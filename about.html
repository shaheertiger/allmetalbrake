Our Story

Why We Built This Site

In 2019, one of our founders spent $180 on a driveway sealer that received glowing reviews from a website that clearly had never applied anything thicker than paint. The sealer turned chalky white within a season, peeled at every expansion joint, and left the driveway looking worse than before. Removing it cost more than the original product.

That experience sparked Home Fix Reviews. We set a simple rule: we only publish scores after we've personally used a product on a real project — minimum one full season of weathering, real substrate conditions, real square footage.

We are not bloggers reviewing home improvement products. We are homeowners and tradespeople who also happen to write. The difference matters.

What We Stand For

Our Editorial Values

No Free Samples

We purchase every product at retail. Manufacturers cannot send us products for review — this eliminates the subtle pressure to be positive about something you didn't pay for.

No Paid Rankings

Our rankings are determined entirely by test scores. An advertiser cannot buy a higher position. The #1 slot goes to the product that earned it in real-world testing.

Transparent Methodology

Every review includes a full breakdown of our test protocol, the conditions used, and the specific measurements taken. You can replicate our tests yourself with the same products.

We Update When Things Change

Manufacturers change formulas between production runs. If a formerly excellent product declines in quality, we update the score and explain exactly what changed.
